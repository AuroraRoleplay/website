Server Rules

It is your responsibility as a player to follow these rules. They are updated often, so make sure to stay up to date!


I) Roleplay Regulations

  • FailRP
    FailRP means failure to roleplay, when you are playing on Aurora and you break character mid session. Ask yourself questions such as “Is this possible in real life?” or “Would my character genuinely do this?”

    Example:
    Doing something absolutely unreasonable or unrealistic whilst in a RP scene.

    First Offense: 1 day ban
    Second Offense: 2 week ban
    Third Offense: Permanent ban
  • NITRP
    NITRP or No Intent to Roleplay means you join Aurora with absolutely zero intent to roleplay.

    Example:
    Joining the server and immediately VDM’ing and RDM’ing people or speaking to people out of character.

    First Offense: 2 week ban
    Second Offense: 30 day ban.
    Third Offense: Permanent ban.
  • In character
    Being In Character means to always follow roleplay, and not FailRP. Being in character includes using RP terminology such as:
    • Server - City
    • Discordia/Email - Discord
    • Tsunami - Server restart
    • Voicebox - Microphone
    • Eyes - Screen/Look at
    • Headpop - Game crash
    • Flex - Click
    • Muscle - Keyboard key
    • Flying In/Out - Joining/Leaving
    • Think about (something) - Type (something)
    • Muscle spasm - Accidental click
    • Bodycam - Clipping software
    • In head - AFK
    • Angel - Staff
    • Local - NPC
    • Lucky Number/PayPal - Your /id
    • Headache - Lagging
    You may not break character at any time. Even in the event of a rule break mid scene, you must complete the scene before calling a staff member. In the event rule breaking occurs live, you still may not break character, ie. using terms such as “VDM” or “RDM.” Be sure to only use these terms in an RP situation; for example, don’t say you have bodycam footage of something if your character is wearing a T-Shirt and Jeans. Also only use these terms when necessary to try to keep players fully immersed.

    First Offense: Warn
    Second Offense: Kick
    Third Offense: Ban (7 day)
    Fourth Offense: Ban (14 day)
    Fifth Offense: Permanent ban
  • NLR
    New Life Rule (NLR) is when your character fully dies in a RP scene (not bleeding out) and must be taken to the hospital. Your character will forget the last 5 minutes of what happened before their full death. NLR can be prevented by having a bodycam and RP’ing looking back at it (although you must have a reason) or somebody telling you in RP what happened.

    Example:
    You get into an RP situation with somebody and you end up fully dying. In an act to get revenge, you go back to the location that you died at, find the person who killed you, and shoot them.

    First Offense: Kick
    Second Offense: 2 week ban
    Third Offense: Permanent ban
  • Fear RP
    Fear for Life is when your character is genuinely feared for their life. Situations like this can only occur when your character is
    Outnumbered (ie 5v1)

    Example: “The Mafia” pulls up on your character with fully automatic rifles and your character is only armed with a knife. Despite the fact your character was outnumbered by 4, you still decided to pull out the knife and attempt to injure one of the “mafia” members. At gunpoint. This example does not apply if your character has a holster and is capable of pulling out a gun fast.

    First Offense: Warn
    Second Offense: 2 week ban
    Third Offense: Permanent ban
  • Exploiting
    Usage of external crosshairs, cheats, modification menus or any sort of non vanilla customization to your game.
    This rule does not apply to graphic enhancement mods such as QuantV or Natural Vision Evolved (NVE) This rule DOES apply to graphic dehancement modifications, such “Boost Packs” or “FPS Packs” which will make your game run significantly better at the exception of giving you an advantage in the sense that you will see less props, have brighter nights, bullet tracers, ect. This includes stretched resolution, which is unrealistic and makes player models appear bigger for an in game advantage

    First Offense: Permanent ban
  • Discrimination and Harassment
    Aurora is a streamer friendly server, in which broadcasters of all kinds play it and enjoy. The Kick Community guidelines article states that any content that is prejudice against the following is not permitted;
    • Race, ethnicity, or national origin
    • Religion
    • Sex, Gender, or Gender Identity
    • Sexual Orientation
    • Disability or Medical Condition
    • Physical Characteristics
    • Veteran Status

    There are zero exceptions to this rule, even if you share the same ethnic origin, religion or sexual orientation as that group, or if it was “just a joke.”

    First Offense: Permanent ban (no appeal)
  • Unrealistic Character Names
    Aurora is a serious roleplay server in which we cannot allow names that would not happen in the real world.
    Example: “Hugh Jass”, “Ben Dover”, “Hugh Janis.”
    For roleplaying as a famous figure, such as a political leader, actor, rich individual or streamer; please contact staff beforehand.

    First Offense: Warn
    Second Offense: Kick
  • Poaching
    Poaching is the act of inviting one player to another server through Discord or inside a city(server).
    Example: “Billy, join my server, it’s brand new and better than Aurora!”

    First Offense: Permanent ban (no appeal)
